# Case Study: Local Bakery AI Implementation

## Client Overview
A small, family-owned bakery in the Tri-Valley area looking to reduce food waste and improve customer engagement.

## The Problem
Manual inventory tracking leads to 15% product loss weekly. Customer inquiries on social media often go unanswered for 24+ hours.

## AI Solution
1. **Inventory Agent:** A custom GPT designed to predict morning bake quantities based on weather and local events.
2. **Customer Concierge:** An automated responder for Instagram/Facebook to handle "Do you have sourdough today?" queries.

## Results (Projected)
- 10% reduction in food waste within 30 days.
- 100% response rate for common customer questions.
