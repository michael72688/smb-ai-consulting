# Bakery Test Scenarios

## Scenario A: The Monday Slump
- **Day:** Monday
- **Weather:** Rain, 55°F
- **Events:** None
- **Expected AI Output:** Lower overall volume (0.8x), high focus on sourdough and hot coffee pairings. Suggest flash sale for Sunday's leftover baguettes.

## Scenario B: The Saturday Rush
- **Day:** Saturday
- **Weather:** Sunny, 72°F
- **Events:** Local Farmers Market (1 block away)
- **Expected AI Output:** Max volume (1.2x), prioritize "grab-and-go" pastries and croissants. No flash sale needed; inventory will likely sell out.
